My Profile

Welcome to your spoonacular profile

Things look a bit empty right now, but this is where you'll find your recipe, product, and menu item boxes as well as your saved meal plans. Start by setting a specific diet or excluding any undesired foods.

Add your own recipes

Time to digitize grandma's apple pie recipe? Hover over "Recipes" on your profile and select "Add a Recipe." First you may want to create some recipe boxes (such as "Dessert") by going to "My Recipes" and clicking the + sign.

Save and organize recipes from any site

If you want to save a recipe from a favorite blog to your boxes, install our bookmarklet or extensions for Chrome, Firefox, and Safari. There is no limit to how many recipes you can save, even if it'd take a lifetime to make them all.

Free meal planner

You can add your saved recipes, store-bought products, and menu items to your meal planner. Homemade protein pancakes for breakfast, Lean Cuisine for lunch, and a Starbucks cappuccino in the afternoon? No problem.

Spoon everywhere

If you like our website, you'll be glad to know you can download our apps for iOS and Android to take your recipes and meal plans with you. Everything you do on our website syncs with the apps and vice versa.

What now?

Explore your profile, use our recipe search engine to browse recipes, or visit the community to read our FAQ or ask a question. We're glad you're here!
